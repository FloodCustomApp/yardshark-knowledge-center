USDA Hardiness Zone Finder

Plant hardiness zones determine which plants will thrive in your climate. Knowing your zone is the first step in plant selection.

Beyond the Zone—Yard Shark's Approach: Here's something cool: hardiness zones tell you about winter cold, but we also think about heat tolerance, humidity, rainfall, and those special microclimates in your yard. That south-facing wall? It might act like you're in a zone warmer. That low spot where frost settles? Zone colder. We geek out on this stuff so your plants don't just survive—they absolutely THRIVE!
